	* Instalar local jar en Maven:
		https://stackoverflow.com/questions/4955635/how-to-add-local-jar-files-to-a-maven-project

		mvn install:install-file
		   -Dfile=<path-to-file>
		   -DgroupId=<group-id>
		   -DartifactId=<artifact-id>
		   -Dversion=<version>
		   -Dpackaging=<packaging>
		   -DgeneratePom=true

		mvn install:install-file

		   -Dversion=1.0
		   -Dpackaging=jar
